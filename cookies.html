Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies

These delicious vegan chocolate chip cookies are quick and easy to make!


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of vegan butter
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 2 vegan eggs
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 2 cups of vegan chocolate chips

Intructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix vegan butter and sugar together.
  3. Add vegan eggs and mix well.
  4. Gradually add flour and stir.
  5. Mix in vegan chocolate chips.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden.
